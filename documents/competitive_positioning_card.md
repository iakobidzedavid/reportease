## 🚀 Messaging by Conversation Stage

### Discovery / Prospecting
**Focus:** Time pain, labor cost

- "Most agencies we talk to spend 6-10 hours every week assembling client reports. What does that look like for your team?"
- "If we could cut that to 30 minutes setup, then fully automated, how would your team use that reclaimed time?"
- Listen for: Current time spent, tools used, pain points with integrations, pricing constraints

### Demo / Evaluation
**Focus:** Speed, quality, ease of use

- Show live dashboard setup (2-3 minutes)
- Show client-facing dashboard (beautiful, white-labeled, real-time)
- Show automated report delivery (scheduled, no manual work)
- Compare side-by-side: ReportEase (30 min setup) vs. Supermetrics/Whatagraph (4-6 week setup)

### Negotiation / Close
**Focus:** ROI, positioning vs. alternatives

- "Supermetrics is $168/user/month; we're $100/user. Your 10-person team saves $680/month."
- "Whatagraph refreshes data every 6-24 hours; we refresh real-time. Your clients always see current metrics."
- "Setup takes 4-6 weeks with competitors; you're reporting within 30 minutes with us."
- "Your brand is on every client dashboard. Competitors don't offer that."

### Post-Sale / Expansion
**Focus:** New client onboarding, additional templates, integrations

- "For each new client, use our templates. Same-day onboarding instead of 2-4 week manual setup."
- "White-label the dashboard so clients think it's YOUR product, not a third-party tool."
- "Real-time data means fewer support tickets; clients don't ask 'why is my data old?'"

## ❌ How to Counter Competitor Arguments

### "Supermetrics is industry standard; everyone knows it."
**Response:** "True, Supermetrics is well-known, but it's priced for enterprise teams. Most agencies we talk to use it partially and fill gaps with manual work. We're built specifically so agencies don't need manual work."

### "Looker Studio is free; why pay for ReportEase?"
**Response:** "Looker Studio is great for quick dashboards, but it's zero automation—every report refresh is manual work. You rebuild it daily for 20+ clients. That's the time drain. We automate all of that. You set it once and it runs forever."

### "Whatagraph has white-labeling. Why ReportEase?"
**Response:** "Whatagraph does white-label, but data refreshes every 6-24 hours; your clients see stale metrics. We refresh real-time. Also, setup is still 2-4 weeks; we're 30 minutes. And their entry price is $249/month; we're $100/user for $1K for your team."

### "We're already using [competitor]. Why switch?"
**Response:** "Switching is hard, I get it. Let's do a 30-day pilot. You'll see 95% of your manual reporting time disappear, and your client dashboards will look 10x better. If that doesn't justify a switch, we didn't deliver."
