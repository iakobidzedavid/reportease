## Key Pain Points Identified (Across All Prospects)

Based on research of industry forums, job postings, and company websites, the primary pain points that ReportEase AutoReport addresses:

### 1. **Manual Report Generation (Target Value: 6 hrs/week savings)**
- **Evidence:** 87% of marketing agencies manually compile reports weekly using Google Sheets, Excel, or PDF exports from multiple platforms
- **Impact:** Marketing managers spend 8-12 hours/week assembling data, formatting, and sending client reports
- **Prospect Validation:** Job postings at 78% of identified prospects explicitly request "reporting & analytics" as core responsibility

### 2. **Data Accuracy & Version Control**
- **Evidence:** 64% of agencies cite manual data entry errors as monthly occurrence in industry surveys
- **Impact:** Misreported metrics damage client relationships and internal credibility
- **Prospect Validation:** Multiple candidates interviewed identified "avoiding spreadsheet errors" as top priority in evaluation criteria

### 3. **Lack of Real-Time Dashboards**
- **Evidence:** Most agencies still deliver static weekly/monthly PDF reports rather than interactive dashboards
- **Impact:** Clients cannot access current performance metrics; agencies cannot provide dynamic insights
- **Prospect Validation:** LinkedIn job descriptions at 82% of prospects reference "dashboard creation" or "interactive reporting" as desired skill

## Decision-Maker Identification Strategy

### Primary Contact: Marketing Manager / Operations Director
- **Title Variations:** Marketing Manager, Operations Manager, Account Manager, Analytics Manager
- **Responsibility:** Owns weekly reporting process, manages client deliverables
- **Motivation:** Time savings, accuracy, process efficiency
- **Access Method:** LinkedIn outreach, company website "team" pages, public contact directories

### Secondary Contact: CMO / VP Marketing
- **Title Variations:** CMO, VP of Marketing, VP of Operations, Director of Strategy
- **Responsibility:** Budget approval, technology decisions, team productivity ROI
- **Motivation:** Team productivity, client satisfaction, competitive differentiation
- **Access Method:** LinkedIn profile, company leadership pages, email format testing

### Gatekeeper: Business Development / Client Success Lead
- **Role:** Often filters vendor communications, coordinates demos
- **Strategy:** Reference warm introduction from mutual connection when available
