**Open Tracking:** Gmail API pixel tracking enabled
- Tracks: First open, number of opens, time of open
- Resolution: Per-recipient, per-email

- All CTAs include UTM parameters: `?utm_source=email_outreach&utm_medium=cold_email&utm_campaign=mvbp_trial&utm_content=[template_name]&email=[recipient_email]`
- Track: Which CTA clicked, time of click, device type

- Replies automatically labeled with "mvbp_responses"
- Sentiment classification: positive, neutral, objection, follow-up-needed

## EMAIL BEST PRACTICES FOR THIS CAMPAIGN

1. **Personalization is required, not optional**
   - Use prospect's first name only (not "Hi Team")
   - Reference their company by exact name
   - If visible, mention their industry/vertical

2. **Keep it conversational**
   - Use 2nd person ("you", "your")
   - Short paragraphs (2-3 sentences max)
   - Avoid corporate jargon ("synergies," "solutions," "best-of-breed")

3. **Lead with their pain, not your solution**
   - Start with "How many hours do you lose..." not "AutoReport saves 6 hours"
   - Make them feel seen before pitching

4. **Make the ask small**
   - "15-minute conversation" not "let's set up a demo"
   - Free trial with no credit card
   - Calendar link, not "let me know when you're free"

5. **Always include social proof**
   - "Used by 20+ US digital marketing agencies"
   - Never claim "trusted by 500+ companies" (not credible for early product)

6. **P.S. lines matter**
   - Include quantified value or secondary pain point
   - Often has higher read-through than main body
