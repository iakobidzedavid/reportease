## Template Strategy Overview

This template suite includes **5 distinct email variants** designed to test messaging resonance across:

1. **Template A:** Time-Savings Hook (Primary contact: Marketing Manager)
2. **Template B:** Accuracy & Data Quality Hook (Primary contact: VP Analytics/Operations)
3. **Template C:** CMO/Executive Hook (Decision-maker: CMO, VP Marketing)
4. **Template D:** ROI Calculator/Free Audit Hook (Economic buyer: CFO, VP Ops)
5. **Template E:** Peer Benchmark Hook (All roles, secondary wave)

Each template is **ready to send with minimal customization.** Highlighted sections `[BRACKETED]` should be customized per prospect using data from the prospect enrichment CSV.

## Subject Line A/B Testing Strategy

**Test pairs to run simultaneously (Tier 1 split into 2 groups):**

| **Test** | **Variant A** | **Variant B** | **Expected Winner** |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Time Savings** | "Cut reporting time from 8 hours to under 2" | "Reclaim 6 hrs/week for strategy" | B (benefits vs. features) |
| **Pain Point** | "Stop wasting $12k/year on manual reporting" | "Your reporting process is hurting retention" | A (financial impact resonates) |
| **Personalization** | "[Name], your agency's reporting problem" | "How [Company] can automate reporting" | A (direct address) |
| **Urgency** | "Reporting automation: Q2 2026 window closing" | "Ready to discuss reporting automation?" | B (softer, less pushy) |

**Run each test for 3-5 days, then consolidate to winning subject line for remaining contacts.**

## Platform Setup & Automation

### Recommended Sending Platforms

- **Email Service:** Gmail (direct) or Outreach.io (automated sequences)
- **CRM Integration:** HubSpot (track opens, replies, meeting scheduling)
- **A/B Testing:** Built into Outreach or manual split (A: 10 prospects, B: 10 prospects)
- **Tracking:** UTM parameters in links (utm_source=email_t1, utm_campaign=reportease_outreach)

### Sequence Automation (Outreach.io Example)

```
Trigger: Email opened or no-reply in 3 days
→ Auto-send Follow-up Email 2

Trigger: Reply received
→ Stop sequence, move to CRM "Sales Conversation" stage

Trigger: 7 days no reply to Follow-up 2
→ Auto-send Follow-up Email 3

Trigger: 10 days no activity
→ Move to "Nurture" bucket, exclude from active outreach
```
