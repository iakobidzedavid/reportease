## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

This document details the complete Google Ads Search campaign for ReportEase targeting digital marketing agencies searching for automated reporting solutions. The campaign is designed to drive trial signups from decision-makers (CMOs, Marketing Managers) at target agencies through high-intent keyword matching and value-proposition-focused ad variants.

**Campaign Objectives:**
- Drive awareness among target agencies actively seeking reporting automation solutions
- Generate trial signups through conversion tracking on landing page form submission
- Collect baseline performance data on cost-per-click (CPC) and cost-per-trial-signup (CPTS)
- Establish CAC benchmark for channel economics analysis
- Test messaging resonance across three value propositions (Time, Accuracy, Visualization)
