### Primary Recommendation: EMAIL-FIRST STRATEGY (Path A)

**Why:**
- Achieves strong 2.19:1 LTV:COCA viability ratio
- Excellent 14.7-month CAC payback (SaaS benchmark: 12-18 months)
- Lowest execution risk (warm outreach within team capability)
- Fastest path to profitability (Month 10)
- Lowest initial investment requirement ($200K vs. $400K+ for balanced)

**Execution:**
- Months 1-3: Build email infrastructure, warm list research
- Months 4-6: Scale to 2,000-5,000 contacts/month, 3-5 paying customers/month
- Months 7-12: Optimize sequences, add content marketing, scale to 50-60 customers/month
- **Year 1 Result:** 330-350 customers, $396K-420K revenue, break-even to +$75K profit

**Secondary: Tiered Pricing (Path B) in Month 7+**
- Introduce 3-tier model after 300+ customers
- Increases ARPU from $1,200 to $1,849
- Expands LTV to $4,075 with expansion revenue
- Improves LTV:COCA ratio to 1.46:1+

### Critical Success Factors

1. **Keep Churn <2.5%** — LTV is most sensitive to churn (±66% impact on LTV)
2. **Optimize Email Conversion** — Response rate >2%, conversion rate >8%
3. **Maintain Gross Margin >75%** — Low COGS through automation
4. **Measure and Act on NPS** — Target customer satisfaction >50 NPS
5. **Validate in First 100 Customers** — Prove churn, conversion, and LTV assumptions
