## Section 3: Engagement Scoring Methodology

### 3.1 Scoring Framework (Detailed Calculation)

Each prospect is scored on three dimensions:

#### Dimension 1: Decision Authority (0-40 points)
- **CMO/VP:** 40 pts (can greenlight purchase)
- **Marketing Director:** 30 pts (can recommend, may approve for small budgets)
- **Marketing Manager:** 20 pts (champion/influencer, requires approval)

#### Dimension 2: Data Quality & Freshness (0-30 points)
- **Email verified + Phone verified:** 30 pts
- **Email verified + Phone unverified:** 25 pts
- **Email unverified + Phone verified:** 20 pts
- **Email unverified + Phone unverified:** 10 pts
- **Bounced/Invalid:** 0 pts

#### Dimension 3: Organizational Fit (0-30 points)
- **Company size 20-50 employees:** 30 pts (sweet spot)
- **Company size 10-19 employees:** 20 pts (smaller, faster decisions)
- **Company size 50+ employees:** 10 pts (larger, slower decisions)
- **Geographic location (CA/TX/NY/FL/IL):** +5 bonus pts
- **Prior engagement signals:** +5 bonus pts

- **90-100 pts:** High Engagement (Tier 1) → Pilot candidates, outreach Week 1

- **30-49 pts:** Low-Medium Engagement (Tier 3a) → Secondary research, Week 4+

## Section 8: Risk Management & Mitigation

### 8.1 Key Risks & Mitigation Strategies

| Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|-----------|--------|-----------|
| **Email deliverability issues** | Low (96% verified) | High (blocks outreach) | Pre-validate list sample (5-10 emails) before campaign launch |
| **Contact role change** | Medium (15-20%) | Medium (wrong decision-maker) | LinkedIn verify top 10 before Week 1 outreach; include secondary titles |

| **Pilot doesn't have time for trial** | Medium (busy managers) | Medium (abandonment) | Offer flexible trial period (2-4 weeks), provide asynchronous support |
| **Prospect hesitates on data privacy** | Medium (GDPR/data concerns) | Medium (delays deal) | Prepare privacy documentation, mention SOC 2 alignment (roadmap) |
| **Wrong vertical messaging** | Low (template-based) | Low (low-tier issue) | Customize email subject lines by vertical; test with 3-person sample first |
