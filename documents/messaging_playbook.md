## SECTION 4: SALES CONVERSATION FRAMEWORK

### **Discovery Call (First 15 Minutes)**

**Goal:** Identify if prospect has pain points #1-3 (time, accuracy, client simplicity).

**Opening Questions:**
1. "How much time do you currently spend on client reporting each week?"
2. "How many people are involved in that process? (E.g., data puller, formatter, approver?)"
3. "Have you ever found an error in a client report after sending it? How often?"
4. "How do your clients currently receive reports? (PDF? Dashboard? Email?)"

**Listen For:**
- ✅ Time over 4 hours/week → Good fit
- ✅ Multiple people involved → Scaling problem
- ✅ Data errors mentioned → Trust/accuracy issue
- ✅ Manual process → Automation opportunity

---

### **Pitch (5 Minutes)**

If prospect has pain points:

"Here's what I'm hearing: Reporting is eating X hours per week, and you have a manual verification step because accuracy matters. That's exactly where most agencies like you are.

We built AutoReport specifically to solve this. Three things:

**1. Speed** — Automated reporting means you save 6 hours/week per person. That's 300+ hours annually you can reinvest in client strategy.

**2. Accuracy** — Real-time data validation. Inconsistencies are flagged before they reach clients. You never send an incomplete report again.

**3. Client Impact** — White-labeled dashboards that look custom-built. Clients see premium. You own the brand relationship.

We're seeing agencies save $X,000 annually just in recovered team time. Want to see how it works with your specific platforms?"

---

### **Demo (10 Minutes)**

**Show:**
1. Quick integration demo (click to connect platforms)
2. Pre-built template library (show 3-4 templates matching their vertical)
3. White-label dashboard example (show custom domain, logos, colors)
4. Scheduled report example (show automation rules, email preview)
5. Real-time validation layer (show error detection in action)

**Emphasize:**
- "5-minute setup, not 2 weeks"
- "White-labeling included, not a $500/month add-on"
- "Real-time data, not stale exports"

---

### **Objection Handling**

**Objection 1: "We're happy with [Google Data Studio / Supermetrics / Whatagraph]"**

Response: "I hear you. Those tools are great for what they do. Question: Are you using their white-labeling feature? Most agencies skip it because it's either expensive or not available. And are your clients seeing the actual Google Data Studio branding, or does it look custom-built?"

[Listen for: If they're not white-labeling, you've found the pain point.]

---

**Objection 2: "Setup will take too long. We don't want another tool to learn."**

Response: "That's the exact problem we solved. Setup is literally 5 minutes—connect your platforms and pick a template. No training required. In fact, most agencies don't need to touch it after setup; it just runs. You're not learning a complex tool; you're checking off automation."

[Offer: Live setup call to prove it takes 5 minutes.]

---

**Objection 3: "We need custom reports for specific clients. Will your templates work?"**

Response: "Great question. We have two approaches: (1) Pick a template and use it as-is—60% of agencies do this because it covers their 80/20 use case. (2) Use our drag-and-drop metric builder to customize. Non-technical users can customize in 2-3 minutes. No code, no training needed. Want to see?"

[Demo the metric builder with their use case.]

---

**Objection 4: "Price seems high compared to [competitor]"**

Response: "I get that. Here's the difference: [Competitor] charges $X for the base tool, then $Y for white-labeling, then $Z for support. We include white-labeling, support, and automated validation in one price. Plus, you're saving 300+ hours annually—what's that worth to your team? The math usually favors us by thousands per year."

[Offer: ROI calculator showing time-savings value.]

---

### **Closing (Next Steps)**

If prospect shows interest:

"Here's what I suggest: Pick one client you currently spend the most time reporting for. We'll set it up in your environment, run it for a week, and show you the exact time savings. If it works, we scale to all clients. If not, no charge. Deal?"
