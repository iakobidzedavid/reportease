## HOW IT WORKS

### **The Time Problem**
Marketing managers at agencies spend **8+ hours per week** on manual reporting:
- 2.5 hours logging into 4-6 platforms and downloading data
- 2.5 hours consolidating and cleaning data in Excel/Sheets
- 1.75 hours analyzing and calculating metrics
- 1.25 hours building branded reports for clients

**Result:** 8 hours/week × 52 weeks × $50/hr (loaded) = **$20,800/year** in lost productivity per person.

### **The AutoReport Solution**
- **Automated data sync** from Google Analytics, HubSpot, Google Ads, Meta, LinkedIn, etc.
- **Real-time dashboards** eliminate copy-paste and formula errors
- **Pre-built client templates** reduce formatting from 1.25 hours to 15 minutes
- **One-click report export** in PDF, PowerPoint, or shared dashboard format
- **Anomaly alerts** surface insights automatically

**Result:** Same reporting quality, **1.5 hours/week instead of 8 hours/week.**

### **The Error Problem**
Manual reporting introduces **8.5% error rate**:
- 4% from data extraction (wrong downloads, duplicates)
- 3% from copy-paste mistakes
- 1.5% from formula errors

**Cost per error:** $100 (direct fix + client rework + trust damage)

**Annual error cost:** 68 errors/year × $100 = **$6,800/year** per marketing manager

### **The AutoReport Fix**
- API-to-API integration eliminates extraction errors (0% vs. 4%)
- No copy-paste workflow eliminates transposition errors (0% vs. 3%)
- Built-in validation rules catch formula errors (0.5% vs. 1.5%)

**Result:** 94% error reduction = **$6,400 saved annually.**
