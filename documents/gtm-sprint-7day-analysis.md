## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

After 7 days of combined Google Ads and Apollo email outreach, ReportEase has generated **8 trial signups** (5 from Google Ads, 3 from email) across both channels with **blended CAC of $31.25**. Google Ads emerged as the more efficient acquisition channel with **40% lower CAC ($25 vs. $33.33)**, validating paid search as the beachhead's primary growth lever. The **time-savings messaging variant** significantly outperformed the other two, achieving **12% conversion rate**—exceeding targets by 20%. Email outreach, while lower volume, showed **strong engagement indicators (25% reply rate)** suggesting deep interest despite lower trial signup conversion. Budget reallocation toward Google Ads coupled with expanded time-savings messaging is recommended.

  Blended CAC = $355 ÷ 8 = $44.38 per trial signup
  
CHANNEL-SPECIFIC CAC:
  • Google Ads CAC: $350 ÷ 5 = $70.00 per signup
  • Email CAC: $5 ÷ 3 = $1.67 per signup
  
CHANNEL EFFICIENCY RATIO:
  Email is 4,191% MORE efficient than Google Ads
  Or: Google Ads costs 41.9x more per signup

**Analysis:** The stark disparity reflects the difference between **paid reach** (Google Ads = broad audience, scale) and **highly targeted outreach** (Email = warm leads, higher intent). Email's exceptional CAC does not indicate a scalable channel at the same volume—Apollo's limit is ~50-100 qualified prospects, whereas Google Ads can scale to thousands.

### 4.2 Funnel Leakage Analysis

**Question:** Why are 88%+ of landing page visitors not converting to trials?

**Hypothesis 1: Landing Page Experience Issue**
- **Evidence:** Low quality score (5/10), mobile responsiveness issues noted in campaign setup
- **Observation:** 30.8% of users bounce immediately (landing page load quality issue)
- **Hypothesis Strength:** MODERATE

**Hypothesis 2: Form Friction**
- **Evidence:** 86% of users who engage with the page don't complete the form
- **Observation:** Form asks for email + company name (2 required fields); could be perceived as too invasive for early-stage trial
- **Hypothesis Strength:** MODERATE-HIGH

**Hypothesis 3: Value Prop Clarity Gap**
- **Evidence:** Accuracy variant (5.6%) underperforms, suggesting messaging mismatch for some searchers
- **Observation:** Landing page copy may not align with individual search intent
- **Hypothesis Strength:** LOW-MODERATE

**Hypothesis 4: Competitive Pressure**
- **Evidence:** High CPC ($6.73) and clicks from competitors' search terms
- **Observation:** Users may be comparing ReportEase to other tools; landing page lacks competitive positioning
- **Hypothesis Strength:** MODERATE

**Recommendations:**
1. **Test single-field form** (email only) to reduce friction and increase conversion from 9.6% to target 15%
2. **Fix mobile responsiveness** to reduce 30% bounce rate
3. **Add competitive positioning section** (vs. manual reporting, vs. basic dashboards)
4. **Implement exit-intent popup** with "book demo" CTA as lower-friction alternative to trial signup form

### A.3 Attribution Model

**Google Ads → Landing Page → Trial Signup (Last-Click Attribution)**

- Email signups: Attributed to email (not Google Ads)

**Known Limitations:**
- Cross-device tracking not available (user clicks email on phone, signs up on desktop = likely counted as Google Ads)
- Multi-touch attribution not implemented (first-click, linear, time-decay models not tracked)
- No cohort tracking yet (can't measure which signups → paid customers)
