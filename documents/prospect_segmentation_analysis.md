## Outreach Strategy by Tier

### Tier 1 Outreach (Immediate — Week 1)
**Goal:** Secure 3-4 pilot customers  
**Channel:** Personalized cold email + LinkedIn outreach  
**Frequency:** 2 touches over 10 days  
**Messaging:**
- Subject: "Cut your weekly reporting time by 75% — [Company Name]'s solution"
- Lead: Quantified value prop (6 hrs saved/week = $24K/year per Marketing Manager)
- CTA: 15-min discovery call + free 2-week trial on top 2 accounts
- Follow-up: LinkedIn connection + 5-day reminder email

**Expected Outcomes:**
- 20% response rate (3/14)
- 40% of responders = qualified pilot (1-2 pilots)
- 35% pilot → customer conversion (Target: 1 customer by end of week 3)

### Tier 2 Outreach (Weeks 2-3)
**Goal:** Secondary pipeline filling  
**Channel:** LinkedIn + email + warm referral (once Tier 1 customer secured)  
**Frequency:** 1 touch initially, then 3 touches over 14 days  
**Messaging:**
- Subject: "How [Peer Company] reduced client report errors by 95%"
- Lead: Case study from Tier 1 customer + compliance angle
- CTA: 20-min demo + 3-week pilot
- Follow-up: Personalized pain-point research email

**Expected Outcomes:**
- 12% response rate (2/16)
- 35% of responders = qualified pilot (0-1 pilot)
- 25% pilot → customer conversion

Hold for later once 2-3 Tier 1 customers are referenceable.

## Risks & Mitigation

| Risk | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|--------|-----------|
| **Low Tier 1 response rate (<10%)** | Delays proof point | Have Tier 2 list ready; adjust messaging based on early feedback |
| **Pilot customer needs non-GA platform** | Scope creep | Pre-pilot interview; document integrations scope (Phase 1 = GA only) |
| **CMO turnover** | Stale contacts | Use Apollo to refresh contact list monthly; cross-reference LinkedIn |
| **Competitor pre-empts** | Market share loss | Move fast; land first pilot in Week 3; get case study by Week 6 |
