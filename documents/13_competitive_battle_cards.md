## BATTLE CARD 1: SUPERMETRICS

### Objection: "We're looking at Supermetrics because it has more connectors and more flexibility."

**Your Reframe:**
"I understand. Supermetrics is the universal connector platform—and if you have a dedicated data team to manage connectors, it's a solid choice. But here's the reality for most agencies: do you have a data engineer on staff who manages Supermetrics?"

**If they say yes:** "Great. In that case, you might need their flexibility. But I'd still ask: are you using all 80+ connectors, or just 3-5? Most agencies use 3-5. That's $600-1,200/month with Supermetrics. We cover those 3-5 for $1,200 per user per year."

**If they say no:** "That's the gap we solve. Most agencies don't have data engineers. So they either pay Supermetrics to sit idle (waste), or they hire consulting. We're built so your marketing manager can own it without IT support. 2-hour setup vs. 4-8 hours. $1,200/user/year vs. $600-1,200/month."

**Cost comparison to share:**
```
TYPICAL 5-USER, 3-SOURCE AGENCY

Supermetrics: $600/month × 3 connectors = $1,800/month = $21,600/year
ReportEase:   $1,200/user/year × 5 users = $6,000/year

Savings: $15,600/year (72% cheaper)

Supermetrics is 3.6x more expensive for the same job.
```

**If they counter: "But we need unlimited connectors for future growth."**

**Your response:** "Growth is smart planning. But here's the thing: growth in data sources usually happens slowly. When it does, we either: (1) add it to our integration roadmap if it's common (Google Ads, Facebook, etc.), or (2) offer API access so you can build a custom integration without paying per-connector fees. No surprise bills. If you ever truly need 50+ connectors, you outgrew ReportEase—and we'll help you migrate to Tableau. But that's not the problem you're solving today."

**Key phrases:**
- "Do you have a data team?" [Listen for answer]
- "3-5 connectors cover 90% of agency workflows"
- "We'll save you $15,600+ per year"
- "API access for non-standard integrations"

**Closing question:**
"If we could set up your top 3 reports in 2 hours (vs. 4-8 hours with Supermetrics) and save you $15,600/year, would that be worth a conversation?"

## QUICK TIPS FOR ALL CONVERSATIONS

### 1. Always ask questions first
"What tools are you currently using? Why did you choose it?" Listen more than you talk.

### 2. Don't bash competitors
Never say "Supermetrics is bad." Instead: "Supermetrics is great if you have a data team."

### 3. Focus on their use case
Not all objections are the same. A freelancer's needs differ from a 30-person agency's needs. Adapt your response.

### 4. Quantify the advantage
"We're better" is weak. "We save you 6 hours/week and $15,600/year" is strong.

### 5. Offer proof
"Let's get you set up in 2 hours. You'll see the difference immediately."

### 6. Never say "we're the best"
Instead: "We're the best fit for [specific use case]."

### 7. Leave the door open
If they're a bad fit for ReportEase, be honest. "Ruler is probably a better fit for your attribution needs." They'll respect your honesty and might refer you later.
