## METHODOLOGY

### Survey Design
- **Population:** 15 high-engagement prospects (from cold outreach campaigns)
- **Engagement Tiers:**
  - Tier 1 (7 prospects): Opened + clicked + filled form or spent 3+ minutes on dashboard
  - Tier 2 (5 prospects): Opened + clicked but no form submission
  - Tier 3 (3 prospects): Opened email only
- **Survey Duration:** 7 days (April 21-28, 2026)
- **Response Incentive:** $50 Amazon gift card for completion
- **Survey Structure:** 5 sections covering current workflows, three pricing scenarios, and company profiling

### Pricing Scenarios Tested

**Scenario 1: Starter — $800/year**
- Automated reports from Google Analytics + 2 ad platforms
- Weekly scheduled PDF reports + basic dashboard
- 3 team members access
- Email support

**Scenario 2: Standard — $1,200/year** [*Our baseline assumption*)
- 5 ad platform integrations (vs. 2)
- HubSpot integration
- Daily scheduled reports (vs. weekly)
- Interactive dashboard with real-time sync
- Unlimited team members (vs. 3)
- Email + Slack support

**Scenario 3: Premium — $1,800/year**
- Custom report template builder
- White-label/reseller options
- API access for custom integrations
- Dedicated Slack channel support
- Quarterly strategy calls with our team
- Usage analytics & optimization recommendations

### Survey Questions
1. **Acceptance:** "Would you pay $X/year for [Tier]?" (Yes/No/Maybe)
2. **Willingness-to-Pay:** "What is the maximum you'd pay?" (Open-ended dollar amount)
3. **Feature Preferences:** "Which features matter most?" (Multi-select checkboxes)
4. **Demographics:** Company size, annual revenue, decision-making authority

## RISK ASSESSMENT

### Survey Limitations
1. **Small Sample Size (n=~8-10)**
   - Not statistically significant for segment analysis
   - Results may vary if scaled to 50+ prospects
   - Recommendation: Use findings directionally; validate with follow-up interviews

2. **Selection Bias**
   - Only high-engagement prospects surveyed
   - May skew toward price-insensitive buyers
   - Actual market may be more price-sensitive
   - Recommendation: Run survey with cold contact list next to compare

3. **Hypothetical Pricing**
   - Respondents predict what they'd pay; actual behavior may differ
   - Stated willingness-to-pay often 20-30% higher than revealed preference
   - Recommendation: Use median WTP, not mean; validate with pilot pricing

4. **No Competitive Comparison**
   - Survey doesn't show competitor pricing alongside ours
   - Responses may be anchored to industry benchmarks
   - Recommendation: Future survey should include competitive context
